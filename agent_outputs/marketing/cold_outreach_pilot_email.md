# Cold outreach email — Pilot recruitment

**Audience:** Finance lead, accountant, owner-operator, or COO at Nigerian SMEs (10–200 employees)
**Channel:** Direct email, 1:1 personalisation
**Format:** Plain text, under 180 words
**Goal:** Book a 20-min call AND collect one anonymised invoice/statement pair (resolves Dev blocker B2 — pilot data needed for matcher validation)
**Send window:** Tuesdays/Wednesdays, 09:30–11:00 WAT

---

**Subject lines (A/B test these three):**
1. Quick question about your month-end at [Company]
2. 10 Lagos SMEs we're testing invoice reconciliation with
3. [First name] — how long does reconciliation take your team?

---

**Body:**

Hi [First name],

I'm [Founder name] — I'm building NovaMesh, a tool that automates invoice-to-payment matching for Nigerian SMEs. Reaching out because [specific reason: "your CFO post last week," "saw [Company] is a 40-person ops business in Lekki," etc.].

One question: when your team reconciles invoices against GTBank/Access/MoMo statements at month-end, how long does it take?

We're piloting with 10 SMEs in Nigeria. The deal:

- You share one month of anonymised invoices + bank statements (we'll send an NDA first).
- We run them through the matcher and walk you through the result.
- If it's useful, six months free access. If not, you've spent 30 minutes and we've learned where it breaks.

Worth a 20-minute call next week? I can do Tuesday 10:00 or Thursday 14:00 WAT.

Either way, thanks for reading.

[Founder name]
NovaMesh — invoice reconciliation for African SMEs
pilot@novamesh.africa

## Follow-up cadence

- **Day 0:** Send email above.
- **Day 4:** Single-line follow-up: *"Bumping this in case it got buried. Worth 20 minutes?"*
- **Day 11:** Soft close: *"I'll stop pestering — if pilot timing isn't right, can I add you to our launch list?"*
- **No further follow-ups.** Three touches max.

## Target list for this cycle (first 10 customers — company priority #2)

Aim for diversity across these segments so the pilot dataset stresses the matcher:

1. 2 × logistics / haulage (high volume, repetitive customers)
2. 2 × B2B services / agencies (lower volume, larger invoices)
3. 2 × wholesale / distribution (many small partial payments)
4. 2 × schools or training providers (recurring monthly billing, mixed cash/transfer)
5. 2 × healthcare / clinics (mixed B2B insurance + B2C payments)

All Lagos, Abuja, or Port Harcourt — we want pilot customers we can visit in person if a data issue needs to be debugged on-site.
